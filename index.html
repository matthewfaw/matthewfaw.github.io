About Me

I'm a first-year Ph.D. student in the ECE department at The University of Texas at Austin, working under Constantine Caramanis and Sanjay Shakkottai.

Lately, I've been interested in problems at the intersection of optimization and bandits.

I completed my B.S.E. in ECE, Computer Science, and Mathematics at Duke University, where I worked under Benjamin Lee.

Publications

    A game theoretic approach to managing sprints in multiprocessors with a shared power supply improves task throughput and efficiency in a variety of Spark workloads.
